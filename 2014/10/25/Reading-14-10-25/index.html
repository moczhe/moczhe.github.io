读《人类理解研究》

Oct 25, 2014

Quote：

When we run over libraries, persuaded of these principles, what havoc must we make? If we take in our hand any volume; of divinity or school metaphysic, for instance; let us ask, Does it contain any experimental reasoning concerning matter of fact and existence? No. Commit it then to the flames: for it can contain nothing but sophistry and illusion.（Hume,An Ecquiry Concerning Human Understanding）

我们常假设：显然的就是客观正确的。
